Frequently Asked Questions

What is OnTrack?

We are a small team, dedicated to helping you book appointments with your GP, view your upcoming appointments, have a zoom session with your Doctor as well as proving up-to-date information regarding COVID-19 restrictions and statistics in your local area.

How can I check when my next appointment is?

In order to view your upcoming appointments, you must sign in and click the appointments tab at the top of the page. If you don't have an account, you can register on the home page.

How can I view local COVID-19 restrictions in my area?

You can use our postcode search to find information about local restrictions and view graphs showing the trend of cases in your area.

Where can I book an appointment to see my GP?

Use our handy calander on the appointments booking page to see when the next available appointment is. If you wish to book an appointment, you must sign in or register for a free account if you don't have one.
