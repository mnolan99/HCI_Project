Local Restrictions - You are in a level 3 area

  • Hospitality
    • Restaurants, cafes, pubs and bars can open indoors and outdoors for the consumption of food and non alcoholic drinks.

  • Shopping
    • All retailers can open providing they follow and have implemented Scottish Government guidance to ensure the safety of customers and staff.
    • The maximum number of people you can meet indoors and outdoors in a restaurant, café, pub or bar is 6 which can be from up to 2 separate households.
  • Socialising (Indoors)
    • You should not meet anyone who is not in your household indoors in your home or in their home.
    • You can meet another household indoors in a public place such as a café or restaurant.
